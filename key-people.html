Samir Biyani

Chartered Accountant, CEO & MD

An industrialist having his roots in an entrepreneurial family, Samir has had exposure and experience across multiple business sectors including Timber, Real Estate, Financial Services, Textiles, Construction and Building Materials, to name a few. This array of knowledge extends to hands-on expertise of planning, setting up, executing and operating a business. Samir has leveraged his penchant for numbers and sharp analytical skills to grow his businesses exponentially.

Samir built further on his passion for finance and depth of corporate knowledge by forging a unique business model that has helped multiple businesses and business owners to be bailed out of distressed situations. His charismatic way of working coupled with the highest degree of professionalism have enabled him to consistently carve out amicable solutions with lenders and financial institutions. Fondly monikered the “Bail Out Man” amongst the known circle, he has mastered the ability of creating intricate working models to design innovative resolution strategies in distressed situations for businesses. Samir’s hallmark of excellence is in his creation of a mutually beneficial scenario for all stakeholders amidst adversity.
