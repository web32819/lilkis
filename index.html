Investment Philosophy

The fund operates on a unique model of specialized and dedicated financing for stressed business entities, ensuring adequate asset-backed securities.

The fund is designed to deliver superior returns with strong emphasis on cash flow while maintaining security and safety of investment.

Investments are based on strong asset base quality, evaluated through deep and thorough analysis mechanisms.

The model addresses funding gaps for businesses with limited access to traditional financial avenues, facilitating revival and resolution.

Opportunities

1

Banking channels are unavailable for many companies due to multiple constraints.

2

Long turnaround time for most NBFCs and Funds.

3

Potential business recovery through resolution plans under insolvency frameworks.

4

Last mile funding to stressed accounts for completion of resolution or settlement with lenders.

5

Non-performing assets and stressed accounts with adequate security coverage in the form of underlying collateral assets.

Exclusion List

  • New age and cash burn businesses.
  • Fraud accounts or susceptible groups.

    power etc.
  • Start-ups or businesses with unproven cash flow track record
    and lack of history.
  • Businesses with closure risk due to geographical
    concentration, industrial or technological obsolescence.
